Authentic Flavors and Local Delights

Milan is not only the capital of fashion and art, but also a city with a unique culinary tradition. Local cuisine combines simplicity and sophistication, where each dish tells the story of the region and its rich culture. On this page, we will introduce you to the most famous dishes of Milan, which are worth trying for everyone who comes to this amazing city. Discover the real flavors of Milan, from classic ossobuco to sweet panettone, and feel part of the Italian gastronomic tradition.

1. Ossobuco and risotto Milanese

These dishes are a must in Milanese cuisine. Ossobuco is a stewed veal shank, while risotto is a delicate saffron dish that perfectly complements the meat.

2. Panettone

A traditional sweet bread that has become a symbol of Christmas. Try it with a cup of espresso.

3. Milanese pizza

In Milan, pizza has its own specialties. It is thin, with a crispy crust, and often with unusual toppings such as strachatella (soft cheese) or fresh artichokes.
