What is Milan famous for?

Duomo Cathedral

The Cathedral of Our Lady of Milan is a jewel of Gothic architecture. Its construction began in 1386 and lasted almost 600 years. Climb to the roof for a breathtaking view of the city.

Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II

This elegant 19th century arcade is one of the oldest shopping centers in the world. Here you'll find luxury brand boutiques and cozy cafes.

Teatro alla Scala

The Milan Opera House is the place where the world's best voices have been heard. La Scala's history dates back to 1778 and it remains a symbol of Italian culture.
